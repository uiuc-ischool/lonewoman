Continuation of the weather of yesterday. Crew variously Employed in ship duty. The Boys trying out the [sea] Elephant blubber on shore. About 4 or 5 years since there was but three women and (__person__Black Hawk as my Boys have named him) __person__the Indian above mentioned left and __person__Capt Robbins (from whom I have the account) calling there with his vessel persuaded two of the females to go [to] __place__St Pedro. the other ran away to the mountains and it is said is sometimes seen by the hunters who still visit __place__the island, but is too wild to be approached. __person__Black Hawk seems to be noncompass and the wounds on his head are the probable cause.

While trying out the blubber to day on the beach the __person__old fellow smelt out what was going on (he is also blind) and after eating at least 5 lbs of the raw stinking material to begin with, he continued to gorge himself with the scraps as they were tried out, till he could contain no more, when he stretched himself out on the rocks with a hot sun shining in his face and after chattering and laughing in his idiotic manner for three hours - he again resumed his station at the Try-pot, where he made a sumptous dinner - and before dark, he was enabled to partake of a supper - which wasin proportion to his Dinner &amp; breakfast. The blubber yielded about 75 Gals of oil.
