He also notes, "that their language is simple, rich and abounding in comprehensive terms; they have many phrases to which <emph>we</emph> have no equivalent. We have thus endeavored to cursorily collect and collate variegated material, and to refer to abundant matter elsewhere existing, for the use of him who will be competent, in the future, to write the <emph>Monarchia Indiana Californicus</emph>, or give elementary food to the __org__California Hiawatha, or the __org__California Auracania. It is to be hoped this son of thunder will not be a thief of other men's brains, like so many of our lazy contemporaries&#8212;not to call them by a worse name.

That portion of __place__North America, between the __place__49th parallel and the __place__Isthmus of Panama, is capable of easily supporting a population of three hundrred [hundred] millions of human souls. It will need such a sea as the __place__Pacific Ocean to display its commerce and its prowess, for the land is illimitable in its resources of climate, soil and productions. The exploration of the precious metals, and their diffusion amongst men, will draw forth new developments of the human character, and every thing old will have passed away. We cannot conceive&#8212;it is not permitted to us to be known or penetrated, by the Divine Creator&#8212;the wonderful nature of the aspect of human affairs one hundred and forty short years from __date__1860&#8212;a mere breath of time. The settlement of all this region will permeate and influence the utmost bound of the Earth and its human races, and we cannot allow ourselves to doubt, for their better happiness and government, and the glory of our Maker. Our great-grandchildren may witness in their old age the marvels of __date__A. D. 2000; and a Californian, in each population of that mighty American empire, will recount to his hearers the perils, the adventures, the workings, the troubles, and struggles, of his ancestors, from __date__A. D. 1848 to __date__A. D. 2000. Everything of the past will have then in __date__A. D. 2000 felt the magnet&#8212;been brought to the touchstone of __place__California. Men will count one, two, ten, thirty, seventy, a hundred, and [a] hundred and fifty years, after the discovery of the gold and silver of __place__California, which set the ball in motion to roll down south over the land, bringing strange changes over the face of Nature, Society, of Commerce and of Government. The prior old-world-things will be dead, dead, dead&#8212;only live in the Books of the multitudinous great libraries of the empire. The work, then, before us, in the <emph>Indiania</emph> of our Continent, is no child's play&#8212;it is the enterprise of giants and prudent soldiers, and, at present, all this is merely in its adolescence and infancy.
